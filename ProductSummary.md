# Summary

This project analyzes Swiggy’s delivery experience from both product and operational perspectives, focusing on improving customer satisfaction, ETA accuracy, delivery efficiency, and customer retention.

The analysis identified several key customer and business challenges, including delivery delays during peak hours, inaccurate ETA predictions, operational inefficiencies, order cancellations, and retention risks.

Using product analytics principles, the project introduced strategic recommendations such as:
- AI-driven ETA optimization
- Smart rider allocation
- Personalized recommendation systems
- Improved delivery tracking
- Retention-focused loyalty strategies

The project also defined core KPIs and operational metrics to measure product success and operational performance.

Overall, this case study demonstrates how product strategy, analytics, operational optimization, and AI-driven solutions can work together to improve user experience and business outcomes in food delivery platforms.
