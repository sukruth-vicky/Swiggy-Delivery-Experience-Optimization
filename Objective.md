# Product Strategy

## Objective

The objective of this strategy is to improve Swiggy’s delivery experience by optimizing operational efficiency, improving ETA accuracy, increasing customer retention, and enhancing the overall product experience.

---

## Strategic Focus Areas

### 1. Delivery Optimization

Improve delivery speed during peak hours by:
- Optimizing rider allocation
- Reducing delivery bottlenecks
- Using dynamic order assignment logic

---

### 2. ETA Accuracy Improvement

Enhance customer trust by:
- Improving delivery time prediction models
- Using real-time traffic and weather conditions
- Updating ETA dynamically during delivery

---

### 3. Customer Retention Strategy

Increase repeat orders by:
- Improving delivery reliability
- Reducing cancellations
- Providing personalized restaurant recommendations
- Offering loyalty-based incentives

---

### 4. User Experience Improvements

Improve overall app experience through:
- Better delivery tracking
- Faster issue resolution
- Simplified checkout experience
- Personalized food recommendations

---

### 5. AI-Driven Optimization

Leverage AI and analytics for:
- Demand forecasting
- Smart rider allocation
- Personalized recommendations
- Delay prediction analysis
