# KPI Framework

## Core Product KPIs

| KPI | Description | Business Impact |
|------|-------------|----------------|
| Average Delivery Time | Measures average time taken to complete deliveries | Impacts customer satisfaction |
| ETA Accuracy | Measures how accurately estimated delivery times match actual delivery times | Builds customer trust |
| Order Completion Rate | Percentage of successfully delivered orders | Measures operational efficiency |
| Cancellation Rate | Percentage of cancelled orders | Impacts revenue and retention |
| Customer Retention Rate | Percentage of returning customers | Indicates long-term product success |
| Repeat Order Rate | Measures how often customers reorder | Reflects customer loyalty |
| Delivery Partner Utilization | Measures rider efficiency during delivery operations | Impacts operational costs |
| Customer Satisfaction Score | Measures overall user satisfaction | Impacts reviews and growth |
