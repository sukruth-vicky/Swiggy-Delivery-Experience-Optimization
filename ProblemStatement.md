# Problem Statement

## Customer Problems

- Late deliveries during peak hours
- Inaccurate ETA predictions
- Frequent order cancellations
- Poor delivery tracking experience
- Inconsistent customer support
- Lack of personalized restaurant recommendations

---

## Business Problems

- Reduced customer retention
- Lower repeat order rates
- High delivery partner inefficiency
- Increased operational costs
- Negative customer reviews
- Competitive pressure from rival platforms

---

## Product Goal

The goal of this project is to improve delivery efficiency, ETA accuracy, customer retention, and overall user experience using product analytics and operational optimization strategies.
