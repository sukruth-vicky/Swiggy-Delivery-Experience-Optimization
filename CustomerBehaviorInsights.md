# Insights

## Customer Behavior Insights

### 1. Peak-Hour Delivery Delays Impact Customer Trust

Customers ordering during lunch and dinner peak hours are more likely to experience delayed deliveries and inaccurate ETA predictions, negatively affecting trust and satisfaction.

---

### 2. Repeat Customers Prioritize Reliability Over Discounts

Returning users are more influenced by delivery consistency and ETA accuracy than promotional offers or discounts.

---

### 3. Working Professionals Expect Faster Resolution During Order Issues

Users ordering during office hours expect quick support resolution and accurate delivery tracking due to time-sensitive ordering behavior.

---

### 4. Younger Users Respond Better to Personalized Recommendations

College students and younger users show higher engagement with personalized restaurant suggestions, trending food recommendations, and discount-driven offers.

---

## Operational Insights

### 5. Rider Allocation Inefficiencies Increase Delivery Delays

Inefficient rider assignment during peak demand periods creates delivery bottlenecks and increases operational pressure.

---

### 6. Restaurant Preparation Time Directly Impacts ETA Accuracy

Delayed restaurant preparation contributes significantly to inaccurate delivery predictions and poor customer experience.

---

### 7. High Cancellation Rates Reduce Customer Retention

Repeated order cancellations negatively impact customer loyalty and increase the likelihood of users switching to competing platforms.
