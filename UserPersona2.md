## Persona 2 — College Student

### Profile
- Age: 18–24
- Occupation: Student
- Ordering Time: Evening & Late Night

---

### Goals
- Affordable food options
- Quick ordering process
- Discounts and offers
- Easy restaurant discovery

---

### Pain Points
- High delivery charges
- Delayed late-night deliveries
- Limited budget-friendly recommendations
- Order cancellations during peak times

---

### Behavioral Traits
- Frequently compares prices
- Highly influenced by offers and discounts
- More likely to try trending restaurants
- High mobile app engagement
